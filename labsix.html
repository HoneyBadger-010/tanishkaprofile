The climate of India can broadly be classified as a tropical monsoon one.
The Indian Meteorological Department (IMD) designates four official seasons: Winter,
from December to early April. Summer or pre-monsoon, from April to June
(April to July in north-western India)
Monsoon or rainy, from June to September.
